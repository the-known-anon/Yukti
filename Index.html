What we aim

We aim to change the way world thinks. And help girls believe that they can be whatever they want to be.

Through Yukti we aim to normalise seeing women skilled in various job-profiles.
User can take a career test, and on the basis of their responses, we shall choose the courses suited to the best of their interests. A list of courses happening around their area would also be available so that they don't miss out
At times, one might lack resources like books and electronic devices (much of a necessity these days for online classes), but one shouldn't miss out on opportunities just because of this.So we are also providing benevolent donors a chance, to help girls and women from all walks of life. They can fill out forms and contact us by telling what they can donate, logistics can be figured and a lot of girls can be helped. Girls who need resources can also contact us.
